The claim 'Apollo 11 landed on the Moon in 1969' is fully accurate. Apollo 11 launched on July 16, 1969, and successfully landed on the Moon on July 20, 1969, making Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin the first humans to walk on the lunar surface.
